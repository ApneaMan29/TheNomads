# Product Backlog We're not estimating points for stories yet, and it's a bit too cumbersome to put tasks here so let's wait on writing those. The product backlog is in priority order with the highest priority at the top. We will be using Pivotal Tracker to make this much easier starting with your team project. But there's nothing magical about using software for this -- co-located teams could use sticky notes on a whiteboard for this.

1 Started U - Daniel As a user or agency with an account, I want to report relevant news and information into a forum. This will allow people to view and discuss over what was reported. People can talk about their experiences and even share useful information to other hikers or agencies. Creates a community of partnership and with people feeling included. This also includes to write a post and submit it for others to see and to be able to see other's posts in one location.
2 Started U - Benjamin As a user or agency with an account, I want to be able to report injuries and store it directly into the database for the purpose of recording and for future references. This feature wil allow users to investigate and search up injuries based on individual, agency, mountain area, nationality, etc. Purposeful for news agencies, cover reports, investigators, school projects, and many other projects.
3 Started U - Andrew As a user or agency (can be a visitor to the site too), I would like to be able to search and find climbers fitting criteria. This is relevant information about who they are, where they are from, a list of their hike expeditions, partnered agencies, and any other relevant information.
